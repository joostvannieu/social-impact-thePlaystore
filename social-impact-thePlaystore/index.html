What we're about

"Pure play", that's what it's all about at The Playground!

Being able to play in a relaxed fashion with family, friends, or strangers, sipping a nice beverage in a warm atmosphere. Digital detoxing in a unique and heartwarming way.

The Playground is the passion-project of its founder Mark Van Achter. Reconnecting people is his goal, and board games are perfect for that. Wherever people meet, creative impulses happen, and The Playground wants to be the ideal setting for that. A creative hub, like a bridge between players, designers and developers. A space where board games can be explored and enjoyed in all their facets!

So, feel free to put yourself at the table, and let yourself be enticed by the uniqueness of a board game in a welcoming setting.

Events hosted at The Playground

Connecting people through board games

We strongly believe that board games can help people come together and connect, and to facilitate this we organize a lot of board-game related events at The Playground.
These events range from weekly club-meetings to prototype testing and game-specific tournaments.

If you feel like hosting a board game-centric event please give us a shout, and we'll help you set it up.
